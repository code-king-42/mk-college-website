About MK College

Our History

Founded in 1973, MK College began as a small liberal arts institution with just 200 students and 15 faculty members. Over the decades, we have grown into a comprehensive college serving over 5,000 students from across the nation and around the world.

Our commitment to academic excellence, innovative teaching methods, and community engagement has made us a respected institution in higher education.

Our Mission

To provide transformative educational experiences that develop critical thinking, foster creativity, and prepare students to be ethical leaders in a global society.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading institution that advances knowledge through teaching, research, and service, while maintaining our commitment to accessibility and community engagement.

Campus Life

At MK College, we believe that education extends beyond the classroom. Our vibrant campus community offers numerous opportunities for students to engage in extracurricular activities, leadership development, and community service.

With over 40 student organizations, Division II athletics, and various cultural events throughout the year, there's always something happening on campus.
