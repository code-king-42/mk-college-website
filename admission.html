Admissions

Admission Process

1

Application

Complete the online application form and submit required documents.

2

Review

Our admissions committee reviews your application materials.

3

Decision

You'll receive an admission decision within 4-6 weeks.

4

Enrollment

If accepted, complete enrollment requirements and register for classes.

Admission Requirements

Undergraduate Programs

  • Completed application form
  • High school transcript or equivalent
  • Minimum GPA of 2.5
  • Standardized test scores (SAT/ACT)
  • Personal statement
  • Two letters of recommendation

Graduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree from accredited institution
  • Official transcripts
  • Minimum GPA of 3.0
  • GRE/GMAT scores (where applicable)
  • Statement of purpose
  • Three letters of recommendation

International Students

  • All standard requirements
  • Proof of English proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS)
  • Financial documentation
  • Passport copy
  • Student visa information
  • Credential evaluation (if required)

Important Dates

Fall Semester

  • Application Deadline: July 15
  • Decision Notification: August 15
  • Orientation: August 25
  • Classes Begin: September 1

Spring Semester

  • Application Deadline: December 1
  • Decision Notification: January 10
  • Orientation: January 20
  • Classes Begin: January 25
